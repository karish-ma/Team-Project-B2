USER INTERACTION WITH @realDonaldTrump

This block chart shows the volume of interaction os tweeter users with Trump's tweets containing migration related terms, including every time he mentions the wall.
The interaction is the total number of 'Hearts' and 'Retweets' all those tweets received per month
Note that each month the interactions add to millions, regardless the fact that the original tweets are a few dozens.
In these graphs it is calculated the ratio Hearts/Tweet and Retweet/Tweet, showing a growing trend of interactions along time.
