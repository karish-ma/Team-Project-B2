Trump and the Wall

As shown in the graph, President Trump has increased the frequency of his tweets with mentions about the wall, most noticeably in the last few weeks. This spike in tweets coincides with his decision to shut down the U.S. government over his demands to have Congress approve funding for the construction of a wall along the US-Mexico border. With 50 mentions, December 2018 has been the month in which Trump has tweeted the most about the “wall” since he launched his Presidential campaign in June of 2015. Source: @realDonaldTrump’s.
