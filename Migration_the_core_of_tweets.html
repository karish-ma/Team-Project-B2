Migration - the core of Trump's Twitter Activity

It is a fact that migration is a relevant topic in Trump’s political communication, but how much of his Twitter activity occurs around this topic.
To analyze the relative frequency of Trump’s comments on migration,
we visualize the total amount of tweets by @realDonaldTrump compared to the percentage of those tweets dedicated discuss migration.

Key Learning: As shown in the graph, Trump is not currently tweeting at his highest level, however, in January 2019 about 33% of his tweets were related to migration issues.
This means that for every 10 tweets coming from Trump’s account,
at least three of them are related to migration.
Although the graph shows parallelisms between the two lines with peaks in similar months,
the visualization of migration-related terms conceives a more consistent trend in which the discussion on migration by Donald Trump has steadily increased over his presidency.
